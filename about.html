Creativity

I consider myself a creative and restless person. I always come up with new project ideas and I enjoy developing my own ideas. It feels like letting your imagination create a new world in real life. This gives me a lot of motivation which I can use for always have something in hand.

That's why I also love learning everything I can. Every time I propose myself a challenge and achieve it, I feel very proud of myself, which takes me to continue working hard on my projects. That's another reason why I chose to be a developer, because you can always learn something new.

Videogames

I love developing videogames and interactive applications. I usually don't play videogames a lot, but I like relaxing playing videogames. Some of my favorite videogames are Minecraft, Pokémon (the old ones), Temtem, Brawlhalla, Monster Hunter, Fire Emblem, Golden Sun...

I generally enjoy random-based videogames (Moonlighter, Soul Knight, ReDungeon), survival videogames (Minecraft, The Forest, Ark...) and creature collecting videogames (Pokémon, Temtem, Invizimals...), and those games inspire me when I develop game ideas.


Colors

Another thing which gets my interest is the light, and one of its properties that I like the most is the color. I get very excited when I see a triangle with rainbow colors which I programmed showing up in a window. From there, I enjoy researching about color spaces, high-dynamic range images, bloom effects...

That's why I also like photography, color theory, optics and optometry. I like playing with the camera settings and get stunning pictures of both nature and people.


Going outside

I'm a very active person, and I feel very connected with nature too. Sometimes I like going for a walk across the field or (if I have the opportunity) across a forest. Forests are the places I like the most when I want to go for a walk and chill in a peaceful place.

I'm also focused on leading a healthy lifestyle; in order to achieve that goal I usually do some workouts which consists of both fitness and calisthenics. I think this two workout styles connect really well with my relationship with nature.
